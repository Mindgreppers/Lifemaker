Why LifeMaker ?

Read in Detail

We have forgotten who we are!

The root cause of Human misery is our division. The idea of me vs other, while in Truth we are all One.

Need for a crowdsourced model to evaluate our Life

In a big World, how do you decide whether to trust a stranger? How do we measure what impact One is making on the world around?

Scope for smarter ways of Natural Selection

The credibility of a person in the network will add weight to their opinion in decision making.

In our System today time and material means money. Substance does not earn sustenance.

Shouldn't people who are generating truly meaningful value for the World, be able to sustain from their good acts?

We are the products, where we should be the owners

What if a responsible community generates, owns and distributes resources, with a motive of growth for every One?

SmokeSignals

Smoke Signals are our tribe’s primary channel to share, express and connect.

Interest Based Connect

Just specify your interests and see SmokeSignal relevant to you. Along with this see all the latest signals being sent by the community in the 'For All' section.

Live

We live in a world where everything is transient and so are SmokeSignal. SmokeSignals on LifeMaker are just like their physical counterpart. They both require wood and last only as long as the wood keeps burning.

Wood

Wood is our medium of exchange and sustainance within and without LifeMaker. It’s what keeps the wheels turning and keeps us fired up. The way to get more wood into your stock is by being valuable. Donating to LifeMaker foundation also gets you more wood.

Thanks

People acknowledge each other’s activities by saying ‘Thanks’. By saying ‘Thanks’ you gift some of your own wood. This ensures genuine appreciation. You can also say ‘No Thanks’ when you find something counter productive, by sacrificing your own wood.

Karma

Karma helps you look back and see the impact that you’ve had through your activities. Your Karma increases when increases when people say 'Thanks' to you increases when you Thank or say No Thanks and decreases on receiving a 'No Thanks'.Over the course of time we expect Karma to stand for one’s credibility and position in the network.
